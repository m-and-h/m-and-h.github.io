Planning Your Stay

We invite you to join us in New Braunfels, Texas!

Getting in

New Braunfels is located right in between two airports. You fly into either Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS) or San Antonio International Airport (SAT).

We'd recommend renting a car if you're planning on staying for the weekend so that you can get out and explore! Uber and Lyft are also available in the area to take you to and from the airport as well as around town.

Where to stay

We have a block of rooms reserved at Schlitterbahn Resort for your convenience. To book, please call (855) 673-6476 and use Group ID 520211 for the Matschek/Palko wedding. All rooms must be reserved by July 17, 2020.

The area also has many alternative options. Standard hotels in New Braunfels include the Fairfield Inn & Suites New Braunfels, Hilton Garden Inn New Braunfels, and Courtyard New Braunfels River Village

Our Story

Matthew and Hannah were both part of the Duke University Class of 2014. They had many mutual friends (our best man and maid of honor used to get together and play guitar!) and had the same favorite study spots but somehow their paths never crossed. They each moved to Seattle after graduation and slowly became part of the same friend group. After three years of friendship, endless bad Bumble dates, and many nights out, they finally realized that there might be something more there. They went on their first date on March 8, 2017. Matt was so nervous he got them very lost and the rest was history. They now live in Bothell, Washington with their dog, Olive, and two cats, Leo and Jules. On weekends, you're most likely to find them trying out new breweries and wineries or thinking up their next house project.
