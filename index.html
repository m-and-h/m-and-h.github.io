Planning Your Stay

We invite you to join us in New Braunfels, Texas!

Getting in

New Braunfels is located right in between two airports. You fly into either Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS) or San Antonio International Airport (SAT).

We'd recommend renting a car if you're planning on staying for the weekend so that you can get out and explore! Uber and Lyft are also available in the area to take you to and from the airport as well as around town.

Our Story

How did you meet?

Matt claims that he knew me in college. I claim that we met when we both moved to Seattle.

Who made the first move?

Matt asked me to drink wine and play Bananagrams at my house. I immediately got nervous and texted my roommates to come home... They all arrived and hung out with us, throwing Matt off of his game. Later that night, I felt bad and asked Matt about his feelings. The rest is history!

How did Matt Propose?

We had each decided that we were going to celebrate Christmas with our respective families. He flew to Virginia, I set off for Texas, and we each kicked off celebrations. On Christmas night, I was opening gifts with my family when the doorbell rang. I walked up to the door and it was Matt! My parents had been prepared for this, so they had our snow machine up and running on the front porch. We went outside and Matt was clearly bracing himself to ask the big question. As he was ramping up, a Suburban full of kids pulled up to the house and proceeded to start playing in our snow machine. We patiently waited and once they left, he pulled out the ring and I said YES! We went inside and had a champagne toast with my very happy and teary family.
